# How to Test Email Content for Free: A Developer's Guide

Testing email notifications is one of the most tedious parts of web development. Setting up SMTP servers, dealing with spam filters, and checking mobile responsiveness across dozens of email clients can take hours.

## The Challenge of Email Testing

When you're building a 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication) system or an order receipt workflow, you need to ensure:
1. **The HTML renders correctly** everywhere.
2. **The plain-text fallback** is readable.
3. **The subject lines** aren't truncated.
4. **The images** have proper alt text.

Traditionally, this required paid services or clunky local setups like MailHog.

## Best Practices for Email Testing in 2026

1. **Inline Your CSS**: Most email clients still struggle with external stylesheets. Use our inline-CSS templates to guarantee compatibility.
2. **Keep it Accessible**: Use high-contrast colors and descriptive alt text for all images.
3. **Test OTP Clarity**: Ensure your verification codes are large and easy to copy on mobile devices.
